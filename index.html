Instructions

1. Write the desired ammount of rows and columns that you want the maze to have, and then click the "Make me a maze" button to generate one.


2. Then click the "Solve the maze" button to see the steps taken from the green square all the way to the red one, this is the process of exploration the program has achieved in order to reach its destination.


3. Finally, you can save your generated maze in a database with the "Save maze" button, and recall it later. You may also fetch previous entries made by other users.


Every maze generated has at least one solution. The program is able to "jump back" to the last known position where there is a crossroad available, in case it has reached a dead-end somewhere along the way.
